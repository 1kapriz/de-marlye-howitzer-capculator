Instructions

The intention is to have a spotter at a set location on the field. They should measure the distance it is from them to the artillery with a rangefind tool/binocs, then take the bearing of the artillery direction, and plug it in. Then, they should take the same measurements but to the desired target. If there is any change in height, try to estimate it but it won't affect anything that much. If you are at the howitzer and measuring distances from it, set Bearing and Distance to target both at 0. Set the bearing to Artillery at 180, and the Distance from Artillery as the distance to your target. All measurements are in studs. Clicks from 0 degrees is how many times you press W to elevate it from flat.
